# Future downloads and delivery

Downloads are deferred. Preview assets are not originals or final delivery files. Future review-copy downloads require private storage, signed URLs, project sharing/access-code checks, lifecycle checks, audit events and retention controls.

Final Delivery Packages will be a distinct Project-level domain with Delivery Files, independent of Reviews and Request References. It will define final/original/preview roles, creator permissions, secure signed access, expiry, revocation, retention and validation. No download controls or partial schema are included in the MVP.
