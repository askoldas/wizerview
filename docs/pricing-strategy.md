# WizerView Pricing Strategy

## Pricing Positioning

WizerView should be friendly to freelancers and small creative teams.

The pricing promise:

**Free forever for real small use. Pay when WizerView becomes part of your client workflow.**

Avoid a short trial that blocks access after 14 days. Freelancers may send a review link to a client and wait longer than that for feedback.

## Core Paywall Principle

Paywall active work, not time.

Good paywall triggers:

- more active projects
- more active reviews
- more storage
- removing WizerView branding
- custom client-facing branding
- comment export
- version history
- more creator seats
- optional PIN protection

Bad paywall triggers:

- blocking existing shared client links
- hiding existing comments
- locking already shared reviews
- forcing payment before the first real client review
- limiting guest reviewers too early

## Free Plan

For the first real client review.

Suggested limits:

- 1 creator user
- 1 active project
- 1 active review link
- up to 3 versions per deliverable
- image/screenshot review
- PDF review
- pinned comments
- pinned discussion and decision notes
- approve/request changes/select a version
- unlimited guest reviewers
- WizerView branding
- 250-500 MB storage
- archived reviews remain viewable

Do not include text draft assets in v1.

## Solo Plan

For freelancers using WizerView regularly.

Suggested price:

**EUR 9-15/month**

Included:

- 1 creator user
- up to 10 active projects
- multiple active review links
- more storage
- multiple deliverables and versions
- unlimited guest reviewers
- pinned comments and replies
- approval/request changes/direction selected
- email notifications later
- remove or reduce WizerView branding
- export comments later
- optional PIN protection

## Studio Plan

For small studios and high-volume freelancers.

Suggested price:

**EUR 19-29/month**

Included:

- 2-3 creator users
- high or unlimited active projects
- more storage
- custom client-facing logo
- client/project spaces
- version history
- comment exports
- priority support

## Agency Plan

For larger teams.

Suggested price:

**EUR 49-79/month**

Included:

- 5+ creator users
- high or unlimited active projects
- larger storage
- advanced client spaces
- team roles
- internal comments later
- approval history
- integrations later
- webhooks or Zapier later

## Access After Limits Or Cancellation

Existing reviews remain viewable.

Existing comments remain visible.

Existing client links should not break.

If needed, reviews can become read-only and new active reviews can be blocked until the user archives older reviews or upgrades.
